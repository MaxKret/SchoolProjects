Mobile Radix Coffeeshop Creates Conversation Around PTSD (Video)

The Daily Texan talks to Cameron Monaghan about 'Shattered' (Video)
On October 31st of 2020, South Austin welcomed a brand new mobile coffee and dispensary shop known as the Radix House to the area. Located at 2701 W. William Cannon Street, Radix House is an establishment dedicated to providing care and resources to those in the community suffering from PTSD by starting conversations with just a cup of coffee. Founder and CEO of the company Roger Pilney says that the purpose of the shop is to create a space for people to discuss their mental health in a safe and welcoming environment.
